Laptop!

I don't expect a laptop but if you were to get me one, here is what do look for. I listed a few traits to look for but the main ones that matter are the Processor and the amount of RAM/Memory.

  • Processor: Intel-i5 is good enough, Intel-i7 is great. Instead or Intel, you may also find AMD or Ryzen. These are good brands too, I'm just not as familiar with their products so ask someone who works there. If you're not sure if you can trust the person you ask, test their knowledge on the matter by asking them how many cores it has and how many gigahertz. You can also ask what cores and gigahertz are, if they stumble over an answer they might not be a reliable source of help.
  • RAM/Memory: Look for a minimum of 8GB of RAM, preferably 12GB. 16GB of RAM(or more) would be totes on fleak.
  • Graphics Card: The Graphics card really isn't as important, but I would like a decent one if possible. If the label says "Integrated Graphics" then that means it does not have one. Look for the words: AMD, Ryzen, NIVIDA, or G-Force but probably ask for help here.
  • Storage: Not as important. As long as there is at least 250GB of internal storage, that's aight. 500GB is better and 1TB is pretty coo
